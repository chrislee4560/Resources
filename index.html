Gripen

Originating from Sweden, the SAAB Gripen E soars above in the skies.

Featured in Splinter Camo

The Gripen can carry a multitude of armaments, being compatible with the U.S. system like JDAM guided bombs, Beyond Visual Range (BVR) missiles like the AIM-120 AMRAAMs, infrared missiles like AIM-9Ms, and bombs GBU-39s

However, the Gripen is also compatible with European systems, being able to carry infrared missiles like the IRIS-T and BVR missiles like the Meteor missile.
